CHILD SAFETY STANDARDS FOR PONDER
==================================

App Name: Ponder
Developer: Jesse Turner
Contact: jesse.turner2021@gmail.com


COMMITMENT TO CHILD SAFETY
--------------------------

Ponder is committed to the safety and protection of all users, especially minors. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form. This document outlines our standards and practices to prevent, detect, and respond to CSAE content and behavior on our platform.


AGE REQUIREMENTS
----------------

- Ponder requires all users to be 13 years of age or older
- Users must verify their age during the registration process
- We reserve the right to terminate accounts that violate this age requirement
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13


ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY
---------------------

Ponder strictly prohibits:

- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
- Content that sexualizes minors in any way
- Grooming behavior or communication intended to exploit minors
- Sharing, distributing, or soliciting exploitative content involving minors
- Any content that endangers the safety or well-being of children
- Sextortion or blackmail involving minors
- Any other form of child exploitation


PROHIBITED CONTENT AND BEHAVIOR
-------------------------------

The following is strictly prohibited on Ponder:

1. Images, videos, or any media depicting child sexual abuse
2. Links to external sites containing CSAE material
3. Text content that describes or promotes child exploitation
4. Attempts to contact minors for inappropriate purposes
5. Sharing personal information of minors without consent
6. Content that normalizes or trivializes child abuse
7. Using the platform to facilitate real-world harm to children


DETECTION AND PREVENTION
------------------------

We employ the following measures to prevent CSAE on our platform:

- Age verification during account registration
- Content moderation and review systems
- User reporting mechanisms for flagging inappropriate content
- Proactive monitoring of platform activity
- Regular review and updates to our safety policies


HOW TO REPORT CSAE CONTENT
--------------------------

If you encounter any content or behavior on Ponder that involves child sexual abuse or exploitation:

1. IN-APP REPORTING:
   - Tap on any answer or comment to access the report menu
   - Select "Report" and choose the appropriate category
   - Provide details about the concerning content

2. DIRECT CONTACT:
   - Email: jesse.turner2021@gmail.com
   - Subject line: "URGENT: Child Safety Report"

3. EXTERNAL REPORTING:
   - National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC): CyberTipline.org
   - Report to local law enforcement
   - Contact the FBI at tips.fbi.gov for serious concerns


OUR RESPONSE TO REPORTS
-----------------------

When CSAE content or behavior is reported or detected:

1. Immediate removal of the content
2. Immediate suspension of the offending account
3. Preservation of evidence for law enforcement
4. Reporting to NCMEC and relevant authorities as required by law
5. Cooperation with law enforcement investigations
6. Permanent ban of the offending user


COOPERATION WITH AUTHORITIES
----------------------------

Ponder will:

- Report all instances of apparent CSAM to NCMEC as required by U.S. law
- Fully cooperate with law enforcement investigations
- Preserve and provide relevant data when legally required
- Respond promptly to valid legal requests from authorities


ENFORCEMENT ACTIONS
-------------------

Violations of our child safety standards will result in:

- Immediate content removal
- Permanent account termination
- Reporting to law enforcement and NCMEC
- Potential legal action

There are no warnings or second chances for CSAE violations.


USER EDUCATION
--------------

We encourage all users to:

- Report any suspicious content or behavior immediately
- Never share personal information with strangers
- Be aware of grooming tactics and manipulation
- Contact authorities if they believe a child is in danger


POLICY UPDATES
--------------

We regularly review and update our child safety standards to address emerging threats and align with best practices. Users will be notified of material changes to this policy.
